About the Summit

The Health Data Summit is a two-day event exploring how data science transforms healthcare delivery and public health. The event features hands-on workshops, poster sessions, and keynote talks from leading researchers.

What you will get

  • Practical workshops on health data pipelines
  • Networking sessions with public health organizations
  • Poster competition with prizes
